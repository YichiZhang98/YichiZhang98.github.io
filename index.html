  • I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Incubation Institute, Fudan University, co-advised by Prof. Yuan Qi and Prof. Yuan Cheng. Before that, I obtained Bachelor's degree and Master's degree from the School of Biological Science and Medical Engineering, Beihang University in 2020 and 2023, advised by Prof. Jicong Zhang.
  • My research interests lies in the interdisciplinary fields of biomedical image analysis, artificial intelligence and healthcare. I have been working on designing data-efficient learning methods for medical image analysis with imperfect learning scenarios such as semi/weakly-supervised learning. Currently, I'm focusing on developing foundation and generalized AI models in medical imaging and deploying AI techniques in real-world healthcare scenarios.

  • I am currently a Ph.D. student at School of Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Incubation Institute, Fudan University. Before that, I obtained Bachelor's degree and Master's degree from the School of Biological Science and Medical Engineering, Beihang University in 2020 and 2023, advised by Prof. Jicong Zhang.
  • My research interests lies in the interdisciplinary fields of biomedical image analysis, artificial intelligence and healthcare. Specifically, I focus on data-efficient learning with imperfect learning scenarios for medical image segmentation such as semi/weakly-supervised learning, and extending them for practical clinical applications. If you have any interest in discussions & collaborators, please feel free to contact.
